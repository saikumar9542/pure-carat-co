Our Story

Crafted for the ones who wear a lifetime.

Pure Carat Co began in a small atelier with a simple belief — that jewellery should be as personal as memory itself. Every ring, every strand, every stone we set is drawn by hand and made by artisans who have practised their craft for generations.

We work with certified 22k and 18k gold, ethically-sourced diamonds, and coloured stones cut to bring out their natural fire. Our pieces are meant to be worn, layered, gifted and passed on — heirlooms in the making.

Thank you for letting us be part of your story.
